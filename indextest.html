  • bxSlider* with vertically centered text

    The trick here is to use display: inline-block and vertical-align: middle for your image and caption. The image also has a margin-right: -100% so the caption gets overlaid on top. Works in Chrome, Firefox and IE (yep - believe it).

    For what ever reason these vertical centering methods just wouldn't work.

    *bxSlider is a wonderful responsive content slider. Use it.
